ABOUT RMB LEGAL SEARCH

RMB Legal Search is a boutique legal recruitment firm focused on matching the top legal talent with roles of all seniority levels at leading law firms, in-house legal departments and non-legal employers.

The RMB Legal Search Difference:

  • A customized, tailored approach to each search
  • Deep market knowledge across practice areas
  • First-hand understanding of and respect for both employer and candidate needs and priorities

Rachel Marx Boufford
Founding Principal

rmb@RMBlegalsearch.com
Direct: +1 (917) 960-3671

Rachel Marx Boufford brings years of experience as a practicing attorney and legal recruiter to placing highly-qualified candidates in roles at law firms and businesses.

After graduating from Harvard Law School, Rachel practiced law at Davis Polk & Wardwell LLP in New York. She then joined Vault, the publisher of law students' most relied-upon guide to legal employers, as Law Editor. In that capacity, she researched and edited Vault's Guide to the Top 100 Law Firms, Guide to Law Firm Pro Bono Programs, and Practice Perspectives: Vault's Guide to Legal Practice Areas. Analyzing quality of life survey responses from thousands of lawyers across the country gave her an unparalleled inside view into the associate experience at the top 100 firms.

Before founding RMB Legal Search, Rachel was a recruiter for Sullivan & Cromwell LLP, where she managed the Firm's lateral recruiting efforts. She most recently served as the Director of Communications for Sullivan & Cromwell.

Rachel is a member of the New York City Bar's Committee on Recruitment and Retention of Lawyers.
