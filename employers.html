THE RIGHT CANDIDATE FOR YOUR ROLE.

Executing an effective and efficient search is a challenge. From developing a clear mandate for the role, to attracting the right candidates and "closing the deal," each touchpoint is essential.

Having managed both lateral recruiting and recruitment marketing for a top law firm, I know first-hand how best to add value to your search. I partner with recruiters to obtain a nuanced understanding of the substantive experience, academic background and cultural values required of each candidate, and I present only the most qualified lawyers for each role.

THE RMB DIFFERENCE

Ahead of the market.

Access to the best talent.
